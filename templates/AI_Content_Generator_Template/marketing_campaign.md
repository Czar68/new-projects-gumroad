### **3. 7-Day Launch Timeline**

**Day 1-3 (Pre-Launch Phase):**
*   **Activity:** Finalize sales page, email sequences, and social media graphics.
*   **Social:** Begin subtle teaser campaign (Post 1 style). Engage with relevant communities (LinkedIn groups, Reddit) *without* hard selling.
*   **Email:** Segment list. Prepare Email 1 for scheduling.

**Day 4-5 (Build-Up Phase):**
*   **Activity:** Ensure all tech (payment gateway, download delivery) is tested.
*   **Social:** Post Reveal/Feature Highlight (Post 2) and Use Case carousel (Post 3). Consider a short LinkedIn or Instagram Live Q&A about AI content trends.
*   **Email:** **Send Email 1** to list.

**Day 6 (Launch Day):**
*   **Activity:** Flip sales page to "Live." Activate discount code.
*   **Social:** Publish celebratory Launch Day posts (Post 5) across all channels. Engage with every comment.
*   **Email:** **Send Email 2** (Launch Announcement) to entire list.
*   **Bonus:** Share real-time social proof (with permission) from early buyers.

**Day 7 (Urgency & Follow-Up):**
*   **Activity:** Monitor sales and customer queries.
*   **Social:** Post Social Proof/Testimonial (Post 4). Run polls/stories asking, "What's your biggest content hurdle?"
*   **Email:** **Send Email 3** (Last Chance) in the evening.
*   **Community:** Be highly active in comments and DMs, providing support and encouragement.

**Post-Launch (Day 8+):**
*   Thank customers publicly.
*   Begin onboarding content (a "Welcome/How to Use" email).
*   Gather testimonials for future marketing.
*   Plan a nurture sequence for those who didn't buy.
