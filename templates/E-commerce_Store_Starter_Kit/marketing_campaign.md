# **E-commerce Store Starter Kit: "Launch in 7 Days" Marketing Campaign**

## **Campaign Concept: "From Idea to Open"**
*Positioning:* Eliminate the tech overwhelm and launch a beautiful, functional online store in one week.
*Target Audience:* Aspiring entrepreneurs, side-hustlers, and small business owners who feel stuck by the complexity of starting an online store.
*Core Message:* Your dream store, unpacked and ready to go.

---

## **7-Day Pre-Launch Timeline**

*   **Day 1-2 (Awareness):**
    *   Finalize all campaign assets (landing page, graphics, ad copy).
    *   Schedule first two social posts (Teaser & Problem).
    *   Begin soft outreach to micro-influencers in the small biz space.

*   **Day 3-4 (Interest):**
    *   Activate email sequence for early-bird list.
    *   Launch Social Posts 2 & 3 (Solution & Features).
    *   Run targeted low-budget ads to the landing page.

*   **Day 5-6 (Desire & Early Access):**
    *   Send Email 2 (Social Proof & Urgency).
    *   Post Social Media 4 (Social Proof/Testimonial).
    *   Enable "Early-Bird" checkout for the first 50 customers (24-hour head start).

*   **Day 7 (LAUNCH DAY - Action):**
    *   Send Email 3 (LAUNCH Announcement).
    *   Post Social Media 5 (Celebration & CTA).
    *   Go live with full product sales page.
    *   Monitor and engage heavily on all platforms.

## **3-Part Email Sequence**

**Subject Line Strategy:** Curiosity ΓåÆ Benefit ΓåÆ Urgency

**Email 1: "Your shortcut is almost here..." (Sent Day 3)**
*   **Preview Text:** The 7-day store launch plan is unlocked.
*   **Body:**
    *   Friendly greeting acknowledging the subscriber's dream to start a store.
    *   Briefly state the problem (complexity, time, cost).
    *   **Introduce the E-commerce Store Starter Kit as the "all-in-one solution."**
    *   Bullet list 3 key components (e.g., "Pre-vetted Shopify/WordPress theme," "Essential Plugin/App Bundle," "Step-by-Step Launch Video Course").
    *   **Call to Action:** "Get on the Early-Bird List for 24-hour head start & a special discount."
    *   **PS:** Hint at a launch day bonus.

**Email 2: "See how Sam did it + Your Early-Bird Invite" (Sent Day 5 - Early-Bird Access Opens)**
