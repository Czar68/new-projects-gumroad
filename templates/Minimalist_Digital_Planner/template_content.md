## ≡ƒôÿ **PLANNER STRUCTURE & INSTRUCTIONS**

### **SECTION 1: FRONT MATTER**
**Page 1: Cover** ΓÇô Clean design with your name/brand.
**Page 2: How to Use This Planner** ΓÇô Quick-start guide.
**Page 3: Year-at-a-Glance** ΓÇô Visual year calendar for major milestones.

### **SECTION 2: YEARLY PLANNING**
**Annual Vision Board**
- *Instructions:* Add 3-5 images representing your creative vision for the year.
- *Example:* "A serene workspace photo," "Screenshot of a goal YouTube subscriber count," "Mood board for your brand aesthetic."

**Goal Setting Framework**
- **Categories:** Creative, Business, Personal, Learning.
- **Template:**
  - Goal: [Specific, measurable goal]
  - Why it matters: [Your motivation]
  - Quarterly milestones: [Breakdown]
  - Success indicators: [How you'll measure]

### **SECTION 3: QUARTERLY PLANNING**
**Quarter Focus Sheet**
- Theme for the quarter (e.g., "Q1: Content Foundation")
- Top 3 priorities
- Key projects
- Learning goals

### **SECTION 4: MONTHLY PLANNING**
**Monthly Spread (2 pages)**
- Left page: Calendar with major deadlines, launches, content drops.
- Right page:
  - Monthly focus word
  - Content buckets (e.g., Tutorials, Behind-the-Scenes, Educational)
  - Income/expense tracker (simple)
  - Monthly review prompts

### **SECTION 5: WEEKLY PLANNING**
**Weekly Spread (2 pages)**
- **Left page:**
  - Time-blocking schedule (7am-9pm, 30-min increments)
  - Top 3 weekly priorities
  - Weekly content checklist
- **Right page:**
  - Daily task lists (Monday-Sunday, minimalist layout)
  - Weekly habits tracker (water, exercise, creative time)
  - Notes/ideas space

### **SECTION 6: DAILY PLANNING**
**Daily Page (1 page per day)**
- Date & focus word for the day
- **Today's Big 3** ΓÇô Only 3 main tasks
- **Creative Block** ΓÇô For notes, sketches, ideas
- **Energy levels** (1-5 scale with notes)
- **End-of-day review:** What worked? What to adjust?

### **SECTION 7: PROJECT TRACKING**
**Project Dashboard**
- Project name, timeline, status
- Next actions
- Resources/links
- Notes

### **SECTION 8: CONTENT CREATION**
**Content Idea Bank**
- Template for capturing ideas across platforms
- Columns: Idea, Platform, Stage (Idea ΓåÆ Draft ΓåÆ Produced ΓåÆ Published), Notes

**Content Calendar (Supplemental Template)**
- Monthly view with slots for: Topic, Platform, Format, Status, Publish Date.

### **SECTION 9: FINANCE TRACKER**
**Simplified Income/Expense Log**
- Date, Client/Project, Amount, Category (e.g., Client Work, Affiliate, Product Sales)
- Monthly total calculation

### **SECTION 10: REVIEW & REFLECTION**
**Weekly Review Template**
- What accomplishments energized me?
- What felt draining?
- What to start, stop, continue?

**Quarterly Review Template**
- Goals progress assessment
- Creative satisfaction rating
- Adjustments for next quarter

## ≡ƒ¢á **BEST PRACTICES FOR USE**

### **Setup Routine (10 minutes weekly)**
1. **Sunday evening:** Review weekly goals, schedule time blocks for deep creative work.
2. **Daily morning (5 min):** Identify your Big 3 tasks.
3. **Daily evening (5 min):** Quick review and energy check.

### **Digital Tool Integration**
- **For Notion users:** Import structure using provided templates.
- **For tablet users:** Use with Apple Pencil/stylus in GoodNotes.
- **For printing:** Select "Fit to page" and use on light gray paper for softer look.

### **Adaptation Tips**
- **Customize sections:** Remove what you don't need.
- **Tag system:** Use color tags for different project types.
- **Hyperlinks:** In PDF, use for quick navigation between sections.

### **Creative Workflow Optimization**
1. **Batch similar tasks:** Group content creation, admin, communication.
2. **Theme your days:** e.g., Mondays for planning, Tuesdays for creation.
3. **Limit context switching:** Use time blocks effectively.

## ≡ƒôñ **IMPLEMENTATION GUIDE**

### **Step 1: Initial Setup**
1. Download all PDF files.
2. Import main planner into your preferred annotation app.
3. Customize the cover with your name/brand.

### **Step 2: Yearly Planning (January or when starting)**
1. Complete Annual Vision Board (add images).
2. Set 3-5 main goals using the Goal Setting Framework.
3. Mark important dates in Year-at-a-Glance.

### **Step 3: Quarterly Planning**
1. Choose a theme/focus for the quarter.
2. Identify key projects.
3. Break down annual goals into quarterly milestones.

### **Step 4: Monthly Planning (last week of previous month)**
1. Transfer key dates from quarterly plan.
2. Determine content focus areas.
3. Set income targets if applicable.

### **Step 5: Weekly/Daily Routine**
1. Follow the setup routine above.
2. Use trackers consistently for 30 days to establish patterns.
3. Adjust templates as needed based on what works for your creative flow.
