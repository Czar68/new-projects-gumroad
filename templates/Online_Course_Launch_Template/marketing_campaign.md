# Marketing Campaign: "Online Course Launch Template"

## Campaign Overview
**Target Audience:** Coaches, consultants, experts, and educators launching their first online course
**Core Message:** Launch your course professionally, efficiently, and stress-free with a proven, done-for-you template system.
**Campaign Goal:** Generate sales through a limited-time launch period.

---

## 7-Day Launch Timeline

### Day 1-2: Pre-Launch Warm-Up
- **Focus:** Build anticipation and curiosity.
- **Actions:**
  - Update website landing page with countdown timer.
  - Schedule all Week 1 social media posts.
  - Finalize email sequences.

### Day 3: Launch Day
- **Focus:** Official announcement and open cart.
- **Actions:**
  - Send "Launch Day" email (Email 1).
  - Post Launch Announcement across all social channels.
  - Enable sales page and checkout.

### Day 4-5: Social Proof & Value Reinforcement
- **Focus:** Overcome objections, showcase benefits.
- **Actions:**
  - Share testimonials (if available from beta users) or highlight template features.
  - Send "Problem Solved" email (Email 2).
  - Host a live Q&A or template walkthrough.

### Day 6: Urgency & Scarcity
- **Focus:** Encourage final decisions.
- **Actions:**
  - Send "Last Chance" email (Email 3).
  - Post about the closing deadline and exclusive bonuses.
  - Highlight what they risk missing.

### Day 7: Cart Close & Transition
- **Focus:** Final push and close cart.
- **Actions:**
  - Final "Doors Closing" social posts.
  - Send a final reminder email 2 hours before close.
  - Close cart, send thank you messages, onboard new customers.
