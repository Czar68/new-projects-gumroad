## 1. Pre-Launch Foundation

### 1.1 Market Validation Checklist
**Instructions:** Complete these steps before creating any course content to ensure demand.

**Validation Steps:**
1. **Identify Your Target Student:**
   - Demographics: Age, profession, income level
   - Psychographics: Goals, pain points, current skills
   - Example: "Aspiring graphic designers aged 25-35 who know basic tools but struggle with client projects"

2. **Validate Course Topic:**
   - Survey existing audience (100+ respondents minimum)
   - Analyze competitor courses (price, content gaps, reviews)
   - Check search volume for topic keywords (Google Trends, AnswerThePublic)
   - **Best Practice:** Identify 3 specific pain points your students mention repeatedly

3. **Test Price Point:**
   - Offer beta access at 30-50% discount to early subscribers
   - Test different price points with small segments
   - **Example:** "Would you pay $197 for a course solving X? $297? $397?"

```

Email 4: Behind-the-Scenes
Subject: What's inside [Course Name]
Content: Sneak peek at modules, student transformation focus

Email 5: Doors Open
Subject: [Course Name] is now open! ≡ƒÜÇ
Content: Clear benefits, link to sales page, urgency element
```

**Launch Sequence (8 emails over 10 days):**
```

Day 3: Student success story

Day 8: Live Q&A invitation
Day 9: Last day for bonus reminder
Day 10: Final countdown (3 emails last day)
```

### 4.2 Sales Page Template
**Structure:**
1. **Headline:** Primary benefit + desired outcome
   *Example:* "Design Logos That Get Approved FasterΓÇöWithout Endless Revisions"

2. **Subheadline:** Who it's for and transformation promise

3. **Video Introduction:** 90-second welcome explaining course

4. **Problem Identification:** 3-5 pain points your ideal student faces

5. **Solution Presentation:** How your course specifically solves each

6. **Instructor Bio:** Credibility building with social proof

7. **Curriculum Breakdown:** Module-by-module with benefits

8. **Student Results:** Testimonials with before/after specifics

9. **Pricing Tiers:** Clear comparison with recommended tier highlighted

10. **Guarantee:** Risk-reversal (30-60 day money back)

11. **FAQ:** Address price, time commitment, prerequisites

12. **Final CTA:** Strong button with urgency language


**Best Practices:**

- Create content that provides value first, sells second (80/20 rule)

- Track metrics: Cost per lead, conversion rate, customer lifetime value
