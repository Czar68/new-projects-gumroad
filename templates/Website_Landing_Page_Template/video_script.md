**[HOOK]**
**Visual:** Quick cut to a sharp, modern laptop. A sleek, animated website landing page materializes on the screen perfectly.
**VO:** "What if you could skip the struggle and launch a stunning, high-converting landing page... in minutes?"

**[PROBLEM]**
**Visual:** Split screen. Left side: a chaotic, poorly designed webpage with low engagement stats. Right side: a person looking stressed, checking a silent phone.
**VO:** "A weak landing page costs you. It loses visitors, kills conversions, and lets your competitors win."

**[SOLUTION]**
**Visual:** The screen clears, revealing the "Website Landing Page Template" logo. The template interface appears, looking clean and intuitive.
