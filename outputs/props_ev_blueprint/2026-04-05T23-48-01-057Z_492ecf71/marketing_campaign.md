# Marketing campaign — Props EV Blueprint

## Core message

Spreadsheet-backed expected value for sports props you can trust and iterate on.

## Audience

Serious sports bettors and analysts who want transparent EV math without leaving a spreadsheet.

## Hooks to test

1. Show the moment you paste tonight’s lines and the sheet surfaces edge and Kelly in one view

2. Contrast chaotic notes with a single sheet where every prop has implied prob and EV percent

3. Highlight copying the master once, then customizing bankroll and fair percentages forever

## Supporting points

- Structured columns for player, stat, book, line, odds, edge, implied probability, EV percent, Kelly units, and notes
- Bankroll cell and fair-percent inputs so sizing stays consistent with your model
- Room to add unlimited rows as you track more props across books
- Clear workflow: copy the master sheet, name your version, and own your data in Drive

## Call to action

Copy the blueprint, name your sheet, and run every prop through the same EV structure this season.
