# Props EV Blueprint

Spreadsheet-backed expected value for sports props you can trust and iterate on.

## Summary

A Google Sheets blueprint that helps you log props, implied probability, edge percentage, Kelly sizing, and recommendations in one place. Make your own copy, set bankroll and fair percentages, and extend rows as your card grows.

## Highlights

- Copy-ready Google Sheet layout built for props EV and bankroll-aware sizing
- Columns align with how sharp bettors think: edge, implied prob, Kelly, and notes per row
- Designed to scale: add players and books without breaking the model
- You keep the copy; the published blueprint stays stable for everyone else
- Works with your existing research—plug in lines and let the sheet organize the rest
