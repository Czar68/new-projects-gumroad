# Props EV Blueprint

## What you get

A Google Sheets blueprint that helps you log props, implied probability, edge percentage, Kelly sizing, and recommendations in one place. Make your own copy, set bankroll and fair percentages, and extend rows as your card grows.

## Who it is for

Serious sports bettors and analysts who want transparent EV math without leaving a spreadsheet.

## How to use your copy

1. Open the blueprint link in a browser while signed into Google.
2. Use **File → Make a copy** and save it to your Drive with a name you recognize.
3. Set your bankroll and fair percentages in the sheet, then add rows for each prop you track.
4. Extend the sheet as you add books, markets, or seasons—your copy stays under your control.

## Feature highlights

- Structured columns for player, stat, book, line, odds, edge, implied probability, EV percent, Kelly units, and notes
- Bankroll cell and fair-percent inputs so sizing stays consistent with your model
- Room to add unlimited rows as you track more props across books
- Clear workflow: copy the master sheet, name your version, and own your data in Drive

## Voice and positioning

Direct, disciplined, and numbers-first. No hype, no hand-waving—just repeatable structure for edge tracking.
