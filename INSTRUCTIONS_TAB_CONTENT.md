## How to use this sheet

### Make a copy (your own version)
1. **File → Make a copy**
2. Name it (e.g. "My EV Tracker 2025")
3. Your copy lives in your Google Drive; only you can edit it.

---

### Enter your inputs (the only cells you need to edit)

- **Fair %** — Your true probability estimate (e.g. 53 for 53%).
- **Line** — The sportsbook line (e.g. 4.5 for O/U).
- **Odds** — American odds (e.g. -110, +120).

The sheet will calculate **Implied %**, **Edge**, **EV**, **Kelly %**, and **Rec** (recommended bet) for you.

---

### Bankroll setup

- Default bankroll in the sheet is **$600** (adjust in the setup section if needed).
- Kelly and recommended units are based on this bankroll. Change it once and the whole sheet updates.

---

### Where to get props (sourcing)

- **TheRundown** — Props, lines, and odds.
- **SGO** — Additional props and markets.

Enter the **Fair %** (your estimate), **Line**, and **Odds** from your research; the sheet does the rest.

---

### Edge explained (Zion example)

- **Fair %** = your true probability (e.g. Zion AST: **53%**).
- **Implied %** = what the sportsbook odds imply.
- **Edge** = Fair % minus Implied % (e.g. **5.38%** = +EV spot).
- **EV** = expected value; **Kelly** = optimal size; **Rec** = recommended action (e.g. **2u BET**).
